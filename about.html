About

Welcome to our Ganesh Brunch Basket. We satrted with an idea to serve Lacto-Vegetarian food in Montreal city with keeping our customers in mind to let them enjoy outdoors while eating our delicious food during summer days. So they could order the Basket online by choosing a location of their choice or Reserve a table to order the Basket at any of our locations they choose along the lakeshore.

We encourage our customers to share their memories like photos or small videos or Reels taken during their experience in our neighborhood by posting on our website including their own music which is an alternate to Karaoke.

On the other hand if they prefer to sit indoors and enjoy our scrumptous food 365 days in a year, they would still have the option to order a Basket and sit and listen to the music posted by our customers or music posted by our own team. They could share their media with all their friends and family visiting our website or at our restaurant locations. Their media is available among all our locations. They will have 1 hr to bring us the basket back once the basket is ready.
